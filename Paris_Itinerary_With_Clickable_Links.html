Disneyland Ride Strategy
  • Walt Disney Studios Ride Strategy
    • Tower of Terror: Start with Crush’s Coaster – This ride has notoriously long lines, so hitting it first thing in the morning is ideal.
    • Crush’s Coaster: 1. Move to Ratatouille: The Adventure – Since it’s nearby, you can quickly check it off your list.
    • Ratatouille: 2. Head to Avengers Assemble: Flight Force – A thrilling coaster that’s worth the adventure.
    • Avengers Assemble: 3. Swing over to Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure – Another Marvel attraction that’s close by.
    • Spider Man: 4. Tower of Terror – A classic Disney thrill ride that usually has a steady queue.
    • RC Racer: 5. RC Racer – A high-speed attraction that’s fun but can have long waits.
    • Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop: 6. End with Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop – It’s a great way to wrap up your route.
  • Disneyland Park Ride Strategy
    • Big Thunder Mountain: Start with Hyperspace Mountain – This ride tends to have long lines, so hit it early.
    • Phantom Manor: 1. Move to Star Tours – It’s nearby and quick to check off.
    • Pirates of the Caribbean: 2. Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast – Another Tomorrowland attraction that’s close by.
    • Indiana Jones: 3. Head to Peter Pan’s Flight – Long waits, so do this earlier.
    • Peter Pan’s Flight: 4. Pirates of the Caribbean – Steady queue classic.
    • Hyperspace Mountain: 5. Indiana Jones and the Temple of Peril – Worth the thrill.
    • Star Tours: 6. Big Thunder Mountain – Aim for a shorter line window.
    • Buzz Lightyear: 7. End with Phantom Manor – It’s nearby and a great wrap-up.
